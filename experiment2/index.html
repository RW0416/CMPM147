Technical

Relation to inspring image: Color: The sky gradient transitions from warm orange to deep purple, inspired by hues sampled from the original image. The mountain silhouettes use dark, atmospheric tones. Primitives: Clouds are rendered as ellipses and the trees are constructed with triangle primitives. Polygons: The mountains are created as single, randomly generated polygons using vertex calls, which gives each peak an organic and varied shape. Composition: Varying the random seed leads to uniquely composed scenes—with different mountain contours, cloud positions, and tree placements—ensuring a fresh landscape on each iteration. Life: Continuous animation via millis() makes the clouds drift across the sky over time, evoking the feeling of a moving scene as if viewed from a passing train, with subtle responsiveness to potential interactive inputs like horizontal mouse movements.
